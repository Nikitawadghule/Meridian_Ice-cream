About Us

Meridian Ice Cream, founded in 1999 in Pune by Nilesh Chavan, is a 100% vegetarian, premium ice cream brand known for using pure milk and natural ingredients. With over 35+ flavors—including popular options like Sitafal, Tender Coconut, and Ferrocher—they offer, chemical-free, and no-added-sugar variants, primarily across Pune.



Highlights

  • Known for authentic tastes, often referred to as "The Real Indian Taste".
  • Focuses on premium, thick, and creamy textures.
  • Offers takeaways and has multiple locations, such as in Camp, Mukund Nagar, and FC Road.
  • Their motto emphasizes quality and natural ingredients.


What makes Meridian ice cream unique? Its regular flavors, crafted with authentic ingredients, ensure that every scoop is a burst of flavor, reminiscent of homemade ice cream that many Indians grew up with. Moreover, the brand's commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made it a household name.
